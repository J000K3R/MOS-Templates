## 🚀 Overview

MOS Templates are designed to integrate directly with the **MOS Hub** and provide:

* ✔️ Discoverable **Docker templates** inside the MOS Hub
* ✔️ Reusable **plugin templates** for MOS extensions
* ✔️ Ready-to-use **docker-compose stacks** for common services
* ✔️ A standardized way to publish and maintain templates

> Goal: Make it easy to share, discover, and reuse MOS-compatible templates via the MOS Hub. 💡
